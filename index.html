Temporal GIS India

Historical Geospatial Evolution Framework

Overview

Temporal GIS India is a framework to integrate historical records with modern geospatial systems. It enables visualization and analysis of how landscapes, economies, and social structures evolve across decades or centuries.

Purpose

This project helps researchers, planners, and policymakers understand long-term spatial transformation. It supports evidence-based planning aligned with national initiatives such as Viksit Bharat.

Key Features

  • Integration of historical and modern geospatial data
  • Time-enabled spatial visualization
  • Analysis of economic, social, and landscape evolution
  • Framework for research and policy planning
  • Support for GIS platforms such as QGIS and PostGIS

Applications

Temporal GIS enables urban planning, infrastructure analysis, environmental monitoring, and long-term development assessment. It provides actionable insights for sustainable national and regional planning.
